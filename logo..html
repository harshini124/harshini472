Tiffany & Co. Logo Usage Guidelines

These guidelines ensure consistency and integrity when using the Tiffany & Co. logo across various mediums.

Correct Logo Usage

Ensure there is adequate spacing around the logo. The minimum required spacing is equal to the width of the logo itself, represented by "X". This helps maintain the logo's visual integrity.

Correct Logo Usage Example: Adequate spacing ensures visibility and clarity.

Incorrect Logo Usage

Avoid the following incorrect logo uses:

  • Do not stretch or distort the logo in any way.
  • Do not change the color of the logo.
  • Do not place the logo on backgrounds that make it difficult to read or blend with.

Incorrect Logo Usage Example: These distortions reduce brand clarity.
